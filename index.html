C390 Portfolio Development module helps to apply what you have learnt in Diploma in Digital Design and Development to showcase your design, web and mobile apps that you have created.

This is a 2-semester module that spans across year 2 semester 2 and year 3 semester 1. Each student will be assigned a supervisor to guide and mentor them in the development of their portfolio. You are expected to put up a portfolio website with a minimum of 2 categories of work and a minimum of six pieces of work per category.

Specifically,

  1. Create a website for your portfolio
  2. On your website, create at least two categories of work (i.e. AR/VR, Design, App Development, etc). Under each category, you should showcase some of your work. Each piece of work should include a detailed design plan, from conceptualisation to the planning and development.
    Take a look at your seniors' work at Exemplary Portfolios for reference. These portfolios serve as a guide, you are encouraged to surpass them.

You will be graded on both the process and the final deliverable of the projects. The process and deliverable are graded based on the 4 components with their weight as shown below.

Source of projects:

  • Work done from your modules
  • Final Year Project related
  • Competition
  • Freelance work/ work done during free time

You should have a clear idea of the different milestones so that you do not fall behind schedule.
